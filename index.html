1

Install MetaMask / Trust Wallet

Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet on your device. Create a new wallet and securely save your seed phrase. Never share it with anyone.

2

Add BNB Smart Chain Network

Open your wallet, go to Settings → Networks → Add Network. Set Chain ID to 56 and RPC to https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org

Buy BNB & Transfer to Wallet

Purchase BNB from Binance, Indodax, or any exchange. Withdraw/transfer to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet address on the BSC network.
